Pneumatic Engine Desk Toy
A compact desk toy powered by a two-cylinder pneumatic engine and driven by human breath, designed to demonstrate key mechanical principles through playful interaction. The engine was CAD-modeled in PTC Creo and features a cam-and-follower intake system for air control, driving a fidget-spinner flywheel to simulate smooth rotational energy.

The project integrates real bearings to reduce friction, fully 3D-printed PLA components, and easily accessible off-the-shelf parts for intuitive assembly. Designed as an educational and novelty item, this toy offers an engaging way to explore engineering concepts through motion.
