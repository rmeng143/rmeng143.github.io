Pneumatic Engine Desk Toy
This desk-sized pneumatic engine toy is powered by human breath and designed for both play and demonstration. It blends novelty and engineering education in one compact form—serving as a fun desktop toy, an educational model for mechanical concepts, or a snap-fit assembly puzzle for hands-on engagement. The engine was fully modeled in PTC Creo and all parts are 3D printed in PLA, with the exception of the bearings, rubber valve balls, and standard fasteners. Each component is designed for tool-free snap-fit assembly with no adhesives required.
