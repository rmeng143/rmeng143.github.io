Ricky Meng – Mechanical Engineer

A desk-sized mechanical toy featuring a two-cylinder pneumatic engine, designed to be powered by human breath. CAD-modeled and prototyped to demonstrate fundamental principles of air-driven motion, energy transfer, and mechanical timing. Includes cam-follower actuation, real bearings for smooth operation, and PLA 3D-printed components.
