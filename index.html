Pneumatic Engine Desk Toy
I made this one mostly as a novelty item that I could use as a gift, but it has some potential to be an educational toy. You can spin it by blowing into the top or flicking the flywheel. I designed the parts in PTC Creo to try something new and 3D printed everything except the bearings, rubber valve balls and bolts. All components slide or snap together so I could assemble a bunch of these consistently.
